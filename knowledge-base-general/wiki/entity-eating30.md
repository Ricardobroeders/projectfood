# Eating30

**What it is:** A free iOS app for tracking 30 plants a week — the closest direct competitor to
Project Food on the core mechanic.

## Summary
Single-player tracker with broad, low-friction logging and a large plant database.

## What we know
- Free core: 500+ plant DB; quick-tap + barcode scanning + Siri voice logging; achievements;
  "eat the rainbow" stats; weekly trends; iCloud sync; fractional plants. Premium adds AI
  ingredient search and prebiotic/polyphenol tracking _(as of 2026-05, [[source-competitor-scan]])_.
- iOS only.

## Relevance to Project Food
Most direct threat on the "effortless logging" pillar: **500+ plants + barcode + voice** vs
Project Food's ~150+ tap-only list. But it appears to be **solo** (no social/leaderboard), which
is Project Food's opening. See [[compare-projectfood-vs-competitors]].

## Contradictions / open questions
- Confirm whether Eating30 has any social features; verify current plant-count and platform
  (Android?) before treating details as fixed.
