# App mockups (May 2026)

**What it is:** Five PNG mockups of the Project Food mobile app (a PWA), showing the main
product surfaces. They reflect product state circa May 2026 (the Stats screen shows weeks
through "May 18–May 24").
**Raw files:** `raw/images/mockup-0.png` … `mockup-4.png`

## Screens captured
- **Home (`mockup-0`):** A large ring showing progress toward 30 (here "38 / 30 — Goal
  reached!"). "Find recipes for your picks" card. "Friends this week" row with per-friend
  counts. "This week's collection" grouped by category (e.g. Vegetable) with chips for each
  plant. Bottom nav: Home · Log · Stats · Top 15 · Social.
- **Avatar / profile customization (`mockup-1`):** Background colour picker, identity option
  (Female / Male / Rather not say), avatar grid, custom avatar option.
- **Stats (`mockup-2`):** Streak (e.g. 27 days) and "Plants tried 98 of 198 total". Weekly
  history bar chart. Per-week list with counts and star when ≥30 (e.g. "May 18–May 24 38/30
  ★"). "Plants tried" progress split by category (Vegetable 35/62, Fruit, …).
- **Log (`mockup-3`):** "Search 150+ plants…" with category filter chips (All, Vegetable,
  Fruit, Herb & spice, Nut & se…). Scrollable list of plants with emoji/illustration and a
  check to add. Confirms the searchable list of 150+ plants from the deck.
- **Profile / settings (`mockup-4`):** User (Ricardo Broeders), Username, Language (EN / NL /
  IT flags), Weekly goal (30 plants), Avatar border (None / Silver), "Install app" (PWA add to
  home screen), Notifications toggle, Sign out.

## How this corroborates the deck
- The Home ring counting to 30 = Pillar 1 ([[concept-brand-pillars]], "the home screen is a
  ring counting to 30").
- Searchable 150+ plant list, no weight/portion/macro fields = Pillar 3 (effortless tap-to-log).
- Leaderboard / "Friends this week" / "Top 15" + Social = the esteem/belonging layers and the
  word-of-mouth model ([[concept-word-of-mouth]]).
- Stats ranks high scores and shows "plants tried" all-time = Pillar 2 (different beats more,
  variety has lasting value).

## Implications for Project Food
- Confirmed surfaces: Home, Log, Stats, Top 15 (leaderboard), Social, Profile. Localized in
  EN/NL/IT. Distributed as an installable PWA.
- The "Find recipes for your picks" card hints at the recipe/recommendation direction.
