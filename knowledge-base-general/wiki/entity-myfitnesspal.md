# MyFitnessPal

**What it is:** A well-known calorie/macro food-tracking app, cited in the brand deck as the
contrast/anti-pattern for Project Food.

## Summary
Used in the deck as shorthand for the kind of food app Project Food deliberately is *not*:
private, predictable, and effortful (calorie/macro logging).

## What we know
- Cited as the example of an app nobody shows off socially: "nobody pulls out MyFitnessPal at
  dinner to show a friend." — _(as of 2026-05, [[source-brand-promise-deck]])_.

## Relevance to Project Food
Defines the negative space Project Food positions against: no calories/macros/portions, no
shame, designed to be visible and shareable (see [[concept-brand-pillars]] Pillars 3–4 and
[[concept-word-of-mouth]]).
