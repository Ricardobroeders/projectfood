# The Believer (target user)

**One-liner:** Someone who believes food is connected to how they feel and wants a simple,
gamified way to act on that belief. (Provisional persona derived from the brand deck's "for
someone in particular" — not yet validated with user research.)

## Profile
- **Context:** Health-curious; sees a link between diet and wellbeing.
- **Goals:** Eat better without dieting; a low-effort, rewarding way to build the habit.
- **Frustrations:** Diet apps that feel like homework — calorie counting, macros, shame
  mechanics, lectures about nutrition science.
- **Behaviors:** Willing to tap-log in the moment; motivated by streaks, leaderboards, novelty,
  and shareable wins.

## What they need from Project Food
- A countable, non-preachy goal (the 30) — _(evidence: [[source-brand-promise-deck]])_.
- Effortless logging and zero guilt — see [[concept-brand-pillars]] (Pillars 3 & 4).
- Social proof and talkable moments — see [[concept-word-of-mouth]].

## Contradictions / open questions
- **This persona is inferred from strategy, not from interviews.** Validate with real user
  research (interviews/surveys) and split into sharper personas if the data supports it.
