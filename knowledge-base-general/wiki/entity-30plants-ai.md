# 30 PLANTS (Eat Better)

**What it is:** A direct "30 plants a week" competitor that uses AI photo analysis to auto-detect
plants in meals.

## Summary
Photo-first logging rather than manual tap/search.

## What we know
- AI photo analysis auto-detects plants in meals; personalized analytics; clean food diary with
  weekly progress; streaks; progress reminders; food-collage generator for sharing _(as of
  2026-05, [[source-competitor-scan]])_.

## Relevance to Project Food
Represents the **AI-photo logging** approach (also ZOE's direction). Project Food's tap-to-log is
faster per item but captures less automatically — a UX bet to watch. The food-collage sharing is
a word-of-mouth feature comparable to Project Food's "talkable moments"
([[concept-word-of-mouth]]).
