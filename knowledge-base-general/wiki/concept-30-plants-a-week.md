# 30 plants a week (the core mechanic)

**In one line:** The brand promise and core product mechanic — *"Eat 30 different plants a
week. Good gut, good life."* The number 0–30 of unique plants per week **is the product**.

## Explanation
Project Food turns gut health into a single countable behaviour: how many *different* plants
you eat in a week, targeting 30. The app measures it directly via tap-to-log. The promise
is deliberately constructed to be:
- **Specific** — 30 plants, one week, countable; the app measures it.
- **True** — it names a behaviour and an aspiration, not a cure.
- **For someone in particular** — see [[persona-believer]].
- **Consistent** — every surface can repeat it without contradiction (login, manifest, home
  ring, advice engine).

The "30 different plants a week" target traces to the gut-microbiome-diversity idea (diversity
of plants → diversity of microbiome). The deck deliberately keeps the science in the
background — _(as of 2026-05, [[source-brand-promise-deck]])_.

## Why it matters to Project Food
It is the spine of the whole product and brand. The four [[concept-brand-pillars]] all exist to
protect and express this mechanic. It also defines the "30/30 week" as a shareable achievement
(see [[concept-word-of-mouth]]).

## Evidence & examples
- Home screen is a ring counting to 30; "Goal reached" celebration at ≥30 — _(as of 2026-05,
  [[source-app-mockups]])_.
- Stats marks any week ≥30 with a star; leaderboard ranks unique plants — _(as of 2026-05,
