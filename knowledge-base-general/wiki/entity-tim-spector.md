# Tim Spector

**What it is:** Professor of epidemiology at King's College London; scientific co-founder of
[[entity-zoe]]; the figure most associated with the "30 plants a week" recommendation.

## Summary
Led the UK arm of the American Gut Project and has popularized plant diversity for gut health
through ZOE and mainstream media.

## What we know
- Led the UK ("British Gut") arm of the American Gut Project _(as of 2018, [[source-30plants-origin-research]])_.
- Co-founder and scientific lead of ZOE _(as of 2026-05, [[source-30plants-origin-research]])_.

## Relevance to Project Food
The intellectual origin of the core mechanic ([[concept-30-plants-origin]]). His prominence is
why "30 plants" has public awareness — useful tailwind, but also why the space is crowded.
