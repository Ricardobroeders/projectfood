# ZOE

**What it is:** A personalized-nutrition company (co-founded by [[entity-tim-spector]]) offering
gut-microbiome/blood testing plus an app; the brand that popularized "30 plants a week."

## Summary
Premium end of the market: science + personalization + at-home testing. The "30 plants"
messaging is central to its content, and its app tracks plant variety toward 30.

## What we know
- Core membership ~£/$9.99/mo; Plus ~$15.99/mo; full program with test ~$294 upfront + $29–39/mo
  _(as of 2026-01, [[source-competitor-scan]])_. A 60%-cheaper £9.99 tier launched Jan 2026.
- ZOE 2.0 uses AI photo food logging and tracks plant variety toward 30; microbiome test is an
  optional add-on _(as of 2026-05, [[source-competitor-scan]])_.

## Relevance to Project Food
The category's premium incumbent and the source of the "30 plants" tailwind. Project Food should
**not** compete on science/personalization (that's ZOE's moat and against Pillar 1) — it should
compete on simplicity, joy, social, and price (free PWA). See
[[compare-projectfood-vs-competitors]].

## Contradictions / open questions
- ZOE is more "adjacent popularizer" than head-to-head rival (different price tier and depth) —
  but it shapes user expectations of what "tracking 30 plants" means.
